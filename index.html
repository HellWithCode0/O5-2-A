II. ORIGIN & RECOVERY (PROMETHEUS LABS)

The operative now known as "Cynical" does not possess a documented life prior to Foundation recovery. They were originally a localized asset of Prometheus Labs, designated as Subject 73 within a highly unethical, black-book initiative known as Project: TETHER.

PROJECT GOAL: Engineer a biological alternative to expensive paratechnology - a human subject capable of acting as an autonomous, living Scranton Reality Anchor and EVE conduit.

Raised in a sterile subterranean testing environment, Subject 73 was subjected to agonizing infusions of raw thaumaturgic energy. This brutal experimentation permanently altered their baseline physiology, effectively granting them living armor (EVE-Saturated Physiology).

To survive the constant sensory assault, their nervous system forcibly adapted, developing a localized anomalous perception that allows them to instinctively "feel" EVE currents and spatial distortions.

Prometheus scientists aggressively weaponized this saturation. In violent testing cycles, Subject 73 was trained in Thaumaturgic Kinematics to manipulate localized fields, moving heavy objects with raw telekinetic force, and projecting disruptive pulses to forcibly siphon the EVE flow of other anomalous subjects.

⚠ RECOVERY EVENT

Project: TETHER concluded when a separate reality-bending experiment went critical. MTF Gamma-13 ("Asimov's Lawbringers") breached the facility in a massive raid, deployed as a compromise due to Nu-7 ("Hammer Down") not being in the area. As Foundation tactical units flooded the corridors, Prometheus thaumaturges initiated a purge protocol, attempting to execute the subjects.

Faced with imminent execution, Subject 73 triggered their primary engineered trait: Quantum Anchoring. They forcefully locked down the spatial geometry of their holding sector, completely nullifying the scientists' lethal spells long enough for Foundation Security to secure them.

III. FOUNDATION FIELD SERVICE (MTF SIGMA-66)

Rather than placing Subject 73 in standard humanoid containment, Site Administration deemed their passive stabilization traits too valuable to waste. For four years, they were deployed as "Special Asset 73" alongside Mobile Task Force Sigma-66 ("Sixteen Tons"), a unit specializing in neutralizing heavy ontokinetic and thaumaturgic threats.

Asset 73 was utilized as a Vanguard. Their role was to breach hostile ritual sites, absorb initial kinetic impacts, and drop their Quantum Anchor to level the playing field so conventional MTF operatives could neutralize reality benders.

They quickly gained a reputation within the unit for prioritizing objective completion over squad survival.

V. OVERWATCH SCOUTING & CURRENT DEPLOYMENT

Subject 73's field career concluded during a containment breach at Site-██. A hostile reality bender infiltrated the Administrative wing, bypassing standard paratech defenses. Asset 73, present on-site for a routine medical debriefing, deployed a Quantum Anchor over the administrative boardroom, stabilizing baseline physics and allowing MTF to neutralize the threat.

Among the personnel secured during this incident was O5-2, "The Aberration."

O5-2, who oversees the Administrative Department (AD), determined that conventional security protocols were inadequate for protecting critical administrative infrastructure. The decision was made to reassign Subject 73 to a specialized administrative role requiring their unique stabilization capabilities.

Subject 73 was transferred from active MTF duty. Personnel records were reclassified to restricted access. Redesignated under the codename "Cynical," they were assigned as O5-2's personal Factotum (O5-2-A).

CURRENT STATUS

Cynical currently operates within O5-2's direct chain of command. Primary responsibilities include high-level logistics coordination, internal security audits, and proximity security during O5 Council proceedings. Authority is limited to O5-2 directives and does not extend to independent command.

Their role functions as a passive security measure, providing localized reality stabilization and administrative support as required.
